Anofet is a brand new application developed by three students who want to gather as much information as possible regarding some popular objects of interest: Restaurants, Albums and Movies. This website allows its visitors to create forms and also fill in forms that describe any existing subject that is included in one of the previously mentioned category. Moreover, it is also possible to create statistics and see their results, according to certain aspects, such as customer preferences based on age groups, nationality and so on. Please enjoy our services and perhaps give us a feedback!
